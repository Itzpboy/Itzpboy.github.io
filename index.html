TO HER

A reserved person by nature,
A girl with great ambitions,
A rare type of person,
Someone who belives in walking alone,
She moves on forward without fear,
She claims to be Unbreakable,
Most people thinks she's a loner,
But she just love being alone,
She may be fierce at times,
But most of it is a Facade,
She's just afraid of hurting those close to her,
She needs to face her fears,
But keeps avoiding it,
There is someone willing to stand by her,
To face it with her,
Still she hesitates afraid of the outcome,
But whatever decision she makes,
The personn will respect it,
She will surely be as expected a CHAMPION.
